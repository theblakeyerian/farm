The chicken operation is the largest hidden labor cost: 973 hrs/yr = 2.67 hrs/day, every single day, 365 days per year. At 8 coops this is non-negotiable — coop moves, fermented feed, egg collection, and water checks cannot be deferred. March is the true peak at 272 hrs (68 hrs/week) — lambing and spring planting collide. Budget for one month of outside lambing help in March (~$2,300) from Year 3 onward.
